Frangollo

Description

Frangollo is a traditional dessert from the Canary Islads. Usually served along with dry fruits and palm honey

Ingredients

  • Mais whole weat
  • Water (or milk)
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Warm the liquid
  2. Put the weat in
  3. Move until it gets creamy
  4. Add raisins
  5. Put on bowls
  6. Fridge until they cool
